Do you offer parent-teacher conferences?

Yes, we hold regular parent-teacher conferences to discuss your child's progress and development milestones.

What is your educational philosophy?

We believe in child-centered learning that respects each child's natural curiosity and individual pace of development.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?

We maintain low teacher-to-child ratios to ensure personalized attention and quality interaction with each student.
